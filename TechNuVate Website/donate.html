Why Give Here

Your money goes directly
to one young person's future.

🎂
Spending your birthday doing good?
A lot of people want their birthday to mean something beyond themselves. Instead of asking for gifts, they want to give one. Donate in celebration and we will display a public thank-you message on your behalf if you want.
🙏
Not comfortable with how your tithe is used?
Many people believe in giving a portion of their income to something greater. If you want that tenth to go somewhere you can see the impact, this is that place. Your tithe funds a real person's education, not a building project you never see.
💡
Think you cannot afford to be philanthropic?
You can. ₦2,000 is not nothing. When twenty people each give ₦2,000, that is one full scholarship. Your small gift, pooled with others, becomes a life-changing opportunity for a young person who cannot afford to enroll alone.
🌎
What exactly does your donation fund?
Every ₦80,000 pool we hit sponsors one student through a full TechNuVate Academy cohort. They get live sessions, mentorship, a certificate, and access to our internship and employment pipeline. The counter updates publicly so you can see the progress.

How It Works

Simple. Transparent. Impactful.

1
You choose an amount
Pick any amount from ₦2,000 upwards. Every donation, big or small, goes into the weekly scholarship pool.
2
The pool fills toward ₦80,000
The progress bar updates in real time. When the pool hits ₦80,000, one student gets fully sponsored into the next TechNuVate cohort.
3
A youth gets empowered
The sponsored student attends live sessions, gets mentored, earns a certificate, and joins our talent pipeline. The counter updates and the next pool begins.
