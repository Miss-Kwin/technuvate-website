Partner pricing is for organisations, churches, schools, or communities enrolling multiple people at once. It is open for a limited window before each cohort. TechNuVate does not offer refunds or cohort deferrals under any circumstances. Once payment is made, your spot is secured for that specific cohort only. If you are unable to attend, you will receive a Badge of Participation rather than a Certificate of Completion. Please make sure you are fully available and committed before enrolling.
A Certificate of Completion is awarded only to students who attend all required sessions, submit all assignments, and meet the minimum standard TechNuVate sets for their track. A Badge of Participation is issued to students who paid and were part of the cohort but did not complete the full programme. Both are issued at the end of the cohort. There are no exceptions to this policy.
