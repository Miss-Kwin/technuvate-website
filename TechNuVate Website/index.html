About TechNuVate

We Are Not Just
Another Tech School.

TechNuVate is a tech organization built to bridge the gap between learning and real opportunity for English-speaking countries and African talent. We train people in practical digital skills, give them real-world project experience, and connect them with businesses that need what they can do.

We exist because where you were born should never determine what you can become. Africa has the talent. We build the bridge.

Yes. TechNuVate Academy and Labs are open to anyone from any English-speaking country worldwide. Our primary focus is Africa and English speaking countries, but we welcome learners from outside Africa who want to learn alongside this community. Not at all. Every course in TechNuVate Academy is designed for people starting from scratch. You do not need to know how to code or have any prior digital experience. The only requirement is a willingness to learn and show up consistently.
Academy is where you learn. You join a cohort, attend live sessions, and earn a certificate. Labs is where you apply what you have learned by working on real project briefs with real mentorship. Studio is where TechNuVate delivers digital services to businesses, and it is also where the best talent from our ecosystem gets to do live client work.
Payment is made directly through the enrollment page using Flutterwave, which accepts card payments, bank transfer, USSD, and mobile money. You can also choose to pay in Nigerian Naira or US Dollars, whichever works best for you. Your spot is only confirmed once payment is completed.
You receive a TechNuVate certificate of completion. If you finish in the top 5 of your track, you earn a free spot in TechNuVate Labs where you work on real client projects and build a professional portfolio. All graduates also join the TechNuVate alumni community.
Yes. TechNuVate partners with organisations, communities, churches, and schools to help their members access courses at a discounted price. Partner organisations receive a set of unique codes that their members use to enroll at the partner pricing tier. Reach out through the contact section below to start a conversation.
No. TechNuVate is for anyone who wants to build a digital skill and use it professionally. Whether you are a student, a working professional looking to switch careers, or someone returning to the workforce, you are welcome. Our cohorts are mixed and we learn better together.
TechNuVate has openings for both team members and tutors. If you have a skill you can teach, visit the Become a Tutor page. If you want to join the core team, visit the Become a Team Member page. Both are available under the Work With Us section in the navigation menu.
TechNuVate does not offer refunds or cohort deferrals under any circumstances. Once payment is made, your spot is locked for that specific cohort only. We ask that you confirm your availability before paying. This policy exists so that every seat in a cohort is occupied by someone who is fully committed.
A Certificate of Completion is only issued to students who attend all required sessions, submit all assignments, and meet the minimum standard TechNuVate sets for their track. A Badge of Participation is issued to students who paid and were part of the cohort but did not fully complete the programme. There are no exceptions to this policy.
The Industrial Training placement is specifically for Nigerian university students who need to complete their school-mandated IT programme. TechNuVate provides a structured digital skills placement, handles your documentation, and works with your institution. You apply first, TechNuVate reviews your eligibility, and payment is only made after you are confirmed eligible. Visit the Labs page to learn more.
