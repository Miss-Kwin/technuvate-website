⚠
This Is a Volunteer Role

TechNuVate tutors are not paid employees. There is no salary, stipend or compensation attached to this role at this stage of our growth. You are giving your time and expertise because you believe in building something that matters for African talent. We want to be completely clear about that before you apply, so that everyone who comes in does so with the right expectations.

What Tutors Do

You Bring the Expertise.
We Bring the Students.

As a TechNuVate tutor, you deliver live sessions, review student work, and give feedback that helps people grow. You do not need to build a curriculum from scratch. We give you a structure and you bring it to life.

🎓
Deliver Live Sessions

Run online classes for your assigned cohort. Sessions are scheduled in advance and agreed around your availability.

💬
Review and Give Feedback

Go through student submissions, give structured feedback, and flag students who are performing exceptionally or struggling.

👥
Mentor and Guide

Be available for questions between sessions. You are not just a teacher. You are someone students can learn from on how to think and work professionally.

📋
Help Shape the Curriculum

Your experience in the field helps us keep what we teach current and relevant. Your feedback on what students need more of shapes the next cohort.

Who We Are Looking For

Experience Over Credentials.
Passion Over Everything.

You do not need a teaching degree. You need to know your craft, communicate clearly, and genuinely care about helping people grow.

💼
Working Professionals

You are currently working in a digital field. You know what the industry actually looks like and you can teach from experience, not theory.

🎓
Past TechNuVate Students

If you went through Academy, built your skills, and are now doing the work, you are exactly the kind of person who can show others how it is done.

🌎
Diaspora Professionals

You live outside Nigeria or Africa but you want to give something back. Teaching remotely for TechNuVate is one of the most direct ways to do that.

What You Get From This

No Salary. But Not Nothing.

We are honest that this is unpaid. But the reasons people stay are real. Here is what tutors genuinely gain from being part of TechNuVate.

🏆
Official Tutor Recognition

You receive a TechNuVate Tutor certification and are listed as a contributor to the programme. This is a real credential you can put on your CV and LinkedIn.

👥
A Network That Grows With You

You join a community of other professionals, mentors, and team members across Africa. Relationships built here have a way of opening doors.

💬
Teaching Makes You Sharper

There is no better way to deepen your own knowledge than having to explain it clearly to others. Tutors consistently say the experience sharpens their own practice.

🌎
Real Impact on Real People

The students you teach will get jobs, build businesses, and build careers because of what you gave them. That is not small. That is the point.

🚀
First Consideration for Paid Roles

As TechNuVate grows, paid opportunities will come. Tutors who have proven themselves in this role are always the first people we consider when those opportunities open up.

⏰
Flexible Commitment

We work around your schedule. You tell us your available hours and we build the timetable around that. Teaching for TechNuVate should not disrupt your life.
