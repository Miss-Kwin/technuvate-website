This Is a Volunteer Role. We Want to Be Direct About That.

There is no salary, stipend, or financial compensation attached to this role at this stage of TechNuVate's growth. You are giving your time because you believe in the mission and you want to be part of building it. That is the only reason to apply. We say this clearly because we respect your time and we want everyone who joins to be here for the right reason.

Open Roles

Where We Need Hands Right Now

These are the specific areas where we need people. Read through each one and apply for the role that fits what you actually do well.

Communications and Content
Communications and Content Associate

For social media marketers, content writers, email marketers, copywriters, and video editors or content creators. If you understand how to make people stop, watch, read, and care, this is your role.

  • Write and schedule content across TechNuVate's social media platforms
  • Write and send newsletters, programme announcements, and updates
  • Write copy for campaigns, launches, and events
  • Keep our tone of voice consistent across every channel and format
Operations
Operations and Coordination Associate

For project managers, business administrators, VAs, and anyone who keeps things running behind the scenes. If you are the person others rely on to make things happen, this is your role.

  • Coordinate between team members, tutors, and students
  • Track programme timelines and flag problems before they become delays
  • Set up and manage tools, workflows, and internal checklists
  • Handle scheduling and logistics for sessions, reviews, and events
Community and Growth
Community and Growth Associate

For community managers, social media managers, and growth-focused people. If you know how to build an engaged online audience and keep people coming back, we need you here.

  • Manage TechNuVate communities on WhatsApp, Instagram, and other platforms
  • Engage prospective students, answer questions, and convert interest to enrolment
  • Plan outreach campaigns to universities, organisations, and online communities
  • Track engagement numbers and share a weekly report with the team
Partnerships
Partnerships and Outreach Associate

For business development people, sales people, PR professionals, and relationship builders. If you are good at starting conversations and turning them into something real, this is for you.

  • Identify and reach out to partner organisations, universities, and communities
  • Onboard and manage partner relationships under our partner pricing programme
  • Research and pursue sponsorship and funding opportunities
  • Represent TechNuVate at virtual events and on relevant online platforms
Design
Product and Graphics Designer

For product designers, UI/UX designers, and graphics designers. If you can make things look good, feel right, and communicate clearly, TechNuVate needs your eye.

  • Design social media graphics, promotional materials, and event visuals
  • Work on UI improvements and visual updates across TechNuVate's web pages
  • Create pitch decks, presentation slides, and documents for the team
  • Maintain brand consistency across all visual assets TechNuVate puts out
Data and Analytics
Data Analytics Associate

For data analysts, BI professionals, and anyone comfortable working with data to find and tell meaningful stories. If you can turn numbers into decisions, we need you here.

  • Track and report on key TechNuVate performance metrics across all three arms
  • Build dashboards and visual reports that give the team clear, actionable insight
  • Analyse student progress, cohort performance, and community growth data
  • Support the team in making data-backed decisions on programme improvements
What You Get From This

Unpaid. Not Unrewarding.

We are honest that this costs you time. Here is what we believe you genuinely get back.

🏆
Official Role Recognition

You receive a TechNuVate team member certification and formal recognition of your role and contributions. Something real to put on your CV and LinkedIn.

🚀
First Consideration for Paid Roles

As TechNuVate grows, paid roles will open. The people inside the organisation doing the work are always the first ones we look at when that happens.

👥
Access to the Network

You work alongside professionals, tutors, and mentors across Africa and beyond. The relationships you build here can shape your career in ways you do not expect.

📋
Real Portfolio Work

Everything you do here is real work on a real organisation. The content you write, the campaigns you run, the community you grow. All of it goes in your portfolio.

🌎
Ground Floor of Something Growing

TechNuVate is building. The people here now will shape what it becomes. That is a different kind of opportunity from joining something that is already big.

🛠
Build Real Organisational Skills

Running operations, managing communities, or handling partnerships at a growing organisation gives you skills that no classroom teaches. You earn these by doing the actual work.
